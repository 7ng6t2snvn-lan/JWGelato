📋 RECIPE #123 - SALTED CARAMEL v4 TEST (4kg)

CARAMEL COMPONENTS:
  1. Sucrose (for caramel): 300g
  2. Water (for caramel): 45g
  3. Butter: 80g

BASE COMPONENTS:
  4. Whole Milk (3.5%): 2000g
  5. Heavy Cream (40%): 600g
  6. Sucrose (for sweetness): 500g
  7. Dextrose: 220g
  8. Skim Milk Powder: 130g
  9. Inulin: 80g ← REDUCED from 150g
  10. Tara Gum: 15g ← INCREASED from 12g
  11. Fine Sea Salt: 10g

TOTAL: ~4047g (before caramel water evaporation)
FINAL: ~4000g (after ~45g water evaporates)

STATS:
  ✓ Fat: 9.4%
  ✓ Sugar: 25.2%
  ✓ MSNF: 8.5%
  ✓ Solids: 44.8%

🔄 WHAT CHANGED: v3 vs v4

| Component | v3 (TOO SOFT) | v4 (FIXED) |
|-----------|---------------|------------|
| Inulin | 150g (3.7%) | 80g (2.0%) ✓ |
| Tara Gum | 12g (0.3%) | 15g (0.4%) ✓ |

WHY THIS FIXES IT:
  • 150g inulin = holds WAY too much water = soft/soupy
  • 80g inulin = creamy but firm = scoopable
  • 15g tara gum = better water binding
  • Result: Rich, creamy, but FIRM texture

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

📝 METHOD (same as v3, just different amounts):

STEP 1 - MAKE CARAMEL:
  - Combine 300g sucrose + 45g water in heavy pan
  - Heat, stirring until sugar dissolves
  - Stop stirring, cook to MEDIUM amber (dark honey color)
  - When nutty smell appears, remove from heat
  - Add 80g cold butter immediately
  - Whisk smooth, set aside to cool slightly

STEP 2 - PREPARE STABILIZER:
  - Mix 15g tara gum + 50g from the 500g sucrose
  - Whisk thoroughly

STEP 3 - MAKE BASE:
  - Heat 2000g milk + 600g cream to 45°C
  - Add caramel (from step 1), whisk smooth
  - Add remaining 450g sucrose + 220g dextrose
  - Add 130g SMP, whisk smooth
  - Heat to 50°C
  - Add tara gum/sugar mix while whisking HARD
  - Heat to 85°C, whisk constantly
  - Hold at 85°C for 2 minutes

STEP 4 - ADD INULIN:
  - Remove from heat
  - Add 80g inulin (NOT 150g!)
  - Blend with immersion blender for 1 minute
  - Cool to 4°C in ice bath

STEP 5 - AGE & CHURN:
  - Refrigerate 12-24 hours
  - Before churning: whisk in 10g salt
  - Churn according to machine
  - Freeze 4+ hours before serving

⚠️ CRITICAL NOTES:

INULIN AMOUNT:
  • Do NOT use 150g - that's what made v3 too soft!
  • Use exactly 80g for proper texture
  • More inulin = softer, won't hold shape
  • Less inulin = firmer but less creamy

TARA GUM AMOUNT:
  • 15g is optimal for this recipe
  • Could go to 18g for even firmer texture
  • Less than 12g = too soft
  • More than 18g = gummy texture

CARAMEL COLOR:
  🟡 Light amber = Maple syrup → Mild flavor
  🟠 Medium amber = Dark honey → RECOMMENDED ✓
  🟤 Dark amber = Mahogany → Risk of bitterness

AVOID "SNOTTY" TEXTURE:
  ✓ Tara gum alone = clean mouthfeel
  ✗ Don't add guar or kappa with this recipe

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

🔧 TROUBLESHOOTING:

STILL TOO SOFT:
  • Reduce inulin to 60g
  • Increase tara gum to 18g
  • Reduce cream to 500g, increase milk to 2100g

STILL TOO HARD:
  • Increase inulin to 100g (max!)
  • Reduce tara gum to 13g
  • Check freezer temp (should be -18°C)

GUMMY/SLIMY TEXTURE:
  • Reduce tara gum to 12g
  • Ensure inulin is fully dissolved during blending

NOT ENOUGH CARAMEL FLAVOR:
  • Increase caramel sucrose to 400g
  • Reduce regular sucrose to 400g
  • Cook caramel slightly darker (careful!)

TOO BITTER:
  • Caramel was too dark
  • Remake with lighter amber color
